Interpretation:
---------------
The model is intentionally biased toward high-risk prediction.
This is a direct result of:

• Aggressive high-risk class weighting (8×)
• Safety-threshold based inference (p > 0.20 → escalate)
• Recall-optimized early stopping during training

As a result:
• No suicidal intent or attempt cases are missed
• Accuracy and precision are lower due to acceptable false positives

This trade-off is EXPECTED and REQUIRED in safety-critical systems.
